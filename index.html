Brian Kim

Being a kind person. Trying to get this PhD done.

Welcome to my side of the world where the centrepoint is curiosity. I am a PhD candidate in Medical Sciences with a primary focus of musculoskeletal radiology, clinical physiotherapist and always trying to be a better communicator. This site is anchored in curiosity about a range of topics; although the focal point is medical imaging, there is always a love for all things philosophy, ethics, physics, sociology and so on.
